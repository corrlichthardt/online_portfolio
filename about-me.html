Hi, I’m a 24 year old web developer / designer, born and raised in New Zealand. After completing my Business Degree at the Auckland University of Technology (AUT), I decided to come to Germany and start an apprenticeship in IT. I have always had an interest in computers, in particular development and after coding my first website to promote my photography skills I just didn‘t want to stop.

I’m a self-taught designer and find the skills I have learnt also complement Frontend Web Development, which I have focused on during my apprenticeship. My ultimate goal is to be super good at Frontend Development and create websites that don’t just conveny information but provide an experience.
